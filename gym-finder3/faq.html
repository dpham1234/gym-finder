Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about finding your perfect gym

How accurate is the pricing information?

We regularly verify pricing information by contacting gyms directly and visiting their facilities. However, prices can change, so we recommend confirming with the gym before signing up. We update our database monthly to ensure accuracy.

Do you charge a fee for using Gym Finder?

No! Gym Finder is completely free to use. We don't charge users any fees. Our revenue comes from partnerships with gyms who want to reach more members, but this never affects our recommendations or comparisons.

Can I book a gym membership through your site?

Currently, we provide information and comparisons to help you make an informed decision. To sign up for a membership, you'll need to contact the gym directly or visit their location. We provide contact information and links to make this process as easy as possible.

How often is the information updated?

We update our database monthly, but we also rely on user feedback. If you notice any inaccurate information, please report it through our contact form and we'll verify and update it within 48 hours.

Are the reviews verified?

We verify reviews to ensure they come from actual gym members. We use a combination of email verification and moderation to maintain the quality and authenticity of our review system. Fake or spam reviews are removed immediately.

My gym isn't listed. How can I add it?

We're always looking to expand our directory! If you know of a gym that isn't listed, please contact us through our contact form with the gym's name, location, and any information you have. We'll research and add it to our database.

Can I compare more than 3 gyms at once?

Currently, our comparison tool allows you to compare up to 3 gyms side-by-side for optimal readability. If you need to compare more gyms, you can create multiple comparison sets or use our advanced filters to narrow down your options.

Do you have a mobile app?

Not yet, but our website is fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices. We're working on a mobile app that will be available in 2025. In the meantime, you can bookmark our site on your mobile browser for easy access.
