Who We Are

Gym Finder Vancouver was born from a simple frustration: finding the right gym shouldn't be complicated. With over 50 gyms across Metro Vancouver, each offering different amenities, pricing, and locations, we saw a need for a comprehensive, unbiased comparison platform.

Our team of fitness enthusiasts and data researchers work tirelessly to keep our information accurate and up-to-date. We visit gyms, verify pricing, check amenities, and gather real member reviews to give you the most complete picture possible.
